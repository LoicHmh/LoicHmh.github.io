Hu Minhao
Researcher at Sensetime Research

I am a graduate student in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). My interests lie in Machine Learning and Medical Imaging for healthcare. I completed my Bachelor Degree in Computer Science at SJTU Paris Elite Institute of Technology in SJTU in 2018. Then I continue my studies for a double diplome program which is supported by both Télécom Paris and SJTU. I have been studied in Paris for two years and worked on projects related to Knowledge Distillation for medical multimodality imaging. I received my Diplôme d'Ingénieur at Télécom Paris in Jan. 2021 as well as my Master Degree at SJTU in Mar. 2021.

I joined smart health care department in SenseTime Research since Apr. 2021 and worked on projects related on fully test-time adaptation for medical image segmentation, as well as weakly- and semi-supervised methods.

Interests: Machine Learning, Medical Imaging
